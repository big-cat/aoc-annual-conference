Lucy Adams

As Managing Director of Firehouse and a founding partner of Disruptive HR, Lucy challenges and supports organisations to bring 21st century approaches to leadership, management organisation and engagement.

Lucy was the BBC’s HR Director during one of its most turbulent periods where during her five year tenure she saw four DGs come and go, oversaw the move to the Salford site and coped with numerous, very public crises including executive pay-offs and the Savile scandal.

Lucy’s significant experience of leading HR and Internal Communications at major corporations spans over 15 years, including law firm Eversheds.
