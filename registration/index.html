Discounts available

AoC member colleges sending two or more delegates on a full fee package will receive a multiple delegate discount as set out below:

  • Second and third bookings will receive a 15% discount
  • Fourth and any subsequent bookings will receive a 20% discount per booking

To receive this discount, please email annualconference@aoc.co.uk including the names of the booked delegates.

If you are an Individual Subscriber, please contact annualconference@aoc.co.uk for details on your fees.

Payment methods

We accept payment by credit card, cheque or BACs transfer. Please note that BACs transfers can only be received once an invoice has been sent. Credit card payments can be made via the website. If you have any problems with your payment please call our Finance Department on 020 7034 9900.

AoC Create is unable to invoice international delegates. All delegates coming from outside the UK will need to make payment online via credit card at the time of registering or they will not gain access to the conference. AoC Create will not issue visa letters unless payment has been received for the booking.

Please note

*The full fee, whether for an AoC member, non-member or affiliate organisation, includes a Gala Dinner ticket. However, if delegates are unable to attend the Gala Dinner or should the Gala Dinner be full at the time of booking they will not be entitled to a reduction in the price of the full conference package due to the substantial discount already applied to this rate. **The full fee cannot be split between different delegates; the same delegate must attend the conference for the three days and the Gala Dinner.

*Accommodation is not included in any package and should be booked via our appointed accommodation agency.

*Please ensure you read carefully our terms and conditions, including our cancellation policy.
