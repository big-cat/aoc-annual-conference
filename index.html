The countdown to the AoC Annual Conference and Exhibition has begun. Bringing together the further education sector to learn, develop, influence, support and exchange information, it is the highlight of AoC’s events calendar.

The conference and exhibition combines keynote speakers, plenary and breakout sessions to inform, stimulate debate and provide the latest news from senior figures in government, education and wider business sectors.

You can also find the latest innovations to support colleges in our varied exhibition – a great way to find out how companies are able to help your college in the future.

5 reasons you should attend in 2016

AoC’s Annual Conference and Exhibition is a valuable opportunity when senior leaders in further education can discover, learn, celebrate and shape the future together.

The conference is a place for professionals to experience and discover everything they need to grow their business – the ideas, the connections and the practical applications shaped from a programme designed to reflect the sector.

  • Learn

    Find out about the latest policy impacting FE directly from those responsible for making them. Hear from leading experts in education and business and learn from experiences inside education and further afield, both nationally and internationally.

  • Network

    Connect with your peers and find out how they tackle the issues facing colleges. Meet new sector leaders and catch up with friends and colleagues.

  • Innovate

    Visit the exhibition hall to uncover the latest innovations in FE. Find out about the new ways these businesses and organisations can support your college.

  • Question

    Have your voice heard by high level politicians and policymakers. The conference provides the platform and opportunity for you to question them directly.

  • Share

    Coming together as a sector from across the country, and internationally, gives us the opportunity to share best practice and learn from our colleagues.
