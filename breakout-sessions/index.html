1.1STEM and innovation

Barking & Dagenham College

STEM is in everything we do, come along to our session to find out how to embed STEM across your curriculum using teaching, learning and assessment through project based and cross curricula approaches to stretch, challenge and engage your students. You will also get an insight into the valuable contribution entrepreneurship and employer engagement makes towards developing the "T-shaped" learner through STEM.

Teaching & learning

1.2Right and wrong: The growing crisis around exam marking

Association of Colleges

There are growing concerns by colleges about the quality of marking at A Level. Last year saw 45,000 corrections and recall of papers and appeals are at record levels and expected to grow again this year. This session will address these concerns. Esher College will be joined by AQA and Ofqual to discuss how colleges can work with the awarding organisations to ensure a high quality of marking for all students.

Quality improvement

1.3STEM and innovation

The Education & Training Foundation

The session will draw on the experience of the Learning Futures Programme in order to show colleges how best to deliver technology projects that improve performance and quality. The focus of the delivery model emphasises the use of sector, and industry, mentors and a strong collaborative community of practice. The Learning Futures model is a structured approach that is flexible and adaptable to the needs of individual colleges and this session will demonstrate its efficacy.

New ways of working
